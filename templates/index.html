Welcome to ISCP

The Intercollegiate Surgical Curriculum Programme (ISCP) is the official platform for surgical and dental training curricula across the United Kingdom and Ireland. It is managed by the Joint Committee on Surgical Training (JCST).

ISCP hosts your training record, workplace-based assessments (WBAs), learning agreements, Annual Review of Competence Progression (ARCP) documentation, and free professional development e-courses.

Use the Virtual Assistant on the right for instant help with common questions, or contact our helpdesk if your query is complex or urgent.

  • Surgical curricula (pre and post-August 2021)
  • Dental surgery curricula (from September 2024)
  • JCST Fellowship curriculum
  • Surgical Advanced Clinical Practitioner (SACP) curriculum
  • MSF Raters portal
  • Free professional e-courses
